The Audi R8

Beginning of a Legend

The second-generation Audi R8 launched in 2015, enhancing everything that made the original great.

Design Evolution

It introduced sharper styling, lightweight construction, and a more aggressive road presence.

Engine & Power

At its heart is a 5.2L V10 engine, naturally aspirated and producing an unforgettable sound.

Performance Specs

  • Power: Up to 602 hp (in V10 Performance)
  • 0–60 mph: Around 3.2 seconds
  • Top Speed: 205 mph (330 km/h)
  • Transmission: 7-speed dual-clutch automatic
  • Drive: Quattro all-wheel drive

Handling & Technology

Features like magnetic ride suspension, carbon-ceramic brakes, and Audi Virtual Cockpit enhance control and immersion.

German Craftsmanship

Built in Germany, the R8 showcases Audi’s dedication to precision engineering and quality.

Legacy

The Gen 2 R8 honors Audi's motorsport heritage while bringing supercar thrills to the road.
